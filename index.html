Features

Everything your team needs,
in one place.

Stop context-switching between your ticketing tool and your repo host. Workvane surfaces the signal — whatever your stack.

Unified Dashboard

All open PRs with their linked ticket statuses in one view. No more tab-juggling between your repo host and your ticketing tool.

Mismatch Detection

Flags when a PR is open but the ticket is Done, or a ticket is In Progress with no open PR. Catch drift before it becomes a problem.

One-Click Transitions

Move tickets through your workflow directly from the PR view. Merge and transition in one action — no context switch required.

Sprint Health

Capacity analysis, unestimated tickets, and velocity warnings. Know if your sprint is on track before the retrospective tells you it wasn't.
